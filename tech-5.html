Composition

Each time you take a photo, you end up making conscious decisions about what items to include or exclude.
You also decide how to arrange the objects that are in your frame.
Composition in photography refers to the position of elements inside the frame and how they interact with each other.

The composition of your photograph impacts the way it’s perceived by others.
When you choose what to fit inside the frame and what you leave out, where to position each element and so on,
you’re capturing a scene with your unique vision.

There are a number of composition techniques that can be used to enhance the picture.
Rule of thirds, leading lines, texture, symmentry etc.
